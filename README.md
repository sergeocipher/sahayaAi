Sahaya AI is a **Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)** solution designed to bridge the digital and linguistic divide for the next billion users in rural India.

By providing a **voice-first, multilingual gateway**, we empower marginalized communities to:

- Access government welfare schemes (G2C)
- Participate in the digital economy (ONDC)
- Operate without needing to read or type
